History

2024

October 2024

(NEW GAME) Heirloom Thief releases.

Releasing on October 4, 2024 for the "Legacy" themed UNCC 2024 49er Kickoff Game Jam, Heirloom Thief follows the medieval peasant Everild on her quest to steal the conquered Heirlooms from the wealthy power-hungry Beasleye family, as rumors circulate about their future plans...

Explore the Beasleye castle at your own peril...

  
 

August 2024

New Group Members: Evan Youssef, Jimmerswagger and Selah
Departure of Group Members: Smit Sakariya and Emily Mapp

Sweet Frontier accepts new members in the form of the programmer Evan Youssef, programmer Jimmerswagger and artist Selah for aid in development following the UNCC 2024 49er Kickstart Game Jam.
Around this time, Smit Sakariya and Emily Mapp leave.

June 2024

Sweet Frontier website created.
Jus Liu takes a Web Application Design course and decides to make this site.
  
 

May 2024

Rogue Science development on indefinite hiatus.

After adding several levels and boss fights for Joey Parker's capstone, development is put on hold.

March 2024

(NEW GAME) Fish releases.

Releasing on March 17, 2024 for the "Level Down" themed UNCC Spring 2024 49hr Game Jam, Fish splashed onto the scene, delighting players with its witty comedy, deep story, and all sorts of twists and turns as the player flops down to the ground level.

  
 

January 2024

(NEW GAME) The Joey's Adventure releases.

Releasing on January 28, 2024 for the "Make Me Laugh" themed 2024 Global Game Jam, The Joey's Adventure tells the fantastic tale of how a single joey was capable of overcoming all odds.

January 2024

Rogue Science development resumes.

Development for an ambitious advanced version of Rogue Science begins for Joey Parker's capstone.

  
   
2023
 

November 2023

(NEW GAME) One Lap and Once in a Lifetime release.

Releasing on November 5, 2023 for the "Only Once" themed UNCC 2023 Fall 49hr Game Jam, One Lap is the first 3D game by Sweet Frontier, telling the story of a racing student when the track starts to take an unexpected turn. Once in a Lifetime, on the other hand is an experience and as such, should not be treated as a game.

September 2023

(NEW GAME) Rogue Science releases.

Releasing on September 28, 2023 for the "Puzzle" themed UNCC 2023 49er Kickoff Game Jam, Rogue Science follows a small telekenetic creature trying to escape the ruins of a lab. However, the little fella overexerts themself when interacting, causing great disorientation, and a rather unique gameplay mechanic

  
 

August 2023

New Group Member: Emily Mapp

Sweet Frontier accepts a new member in the form of the artist Emily Mapp for aid in development for the UNCC 2023 49er Kickoff Game Jam.

July 2023

Sweet Frontier Logo created

Originally from a game that would never be released, on July 9, 2023, the Dragonfruit Dragon became the new primary mascot, overthrowing the Apple Cowboy.

  
 

April 2023

(NEW GAME) OrbitaLoop releases.

Releasing on April 23, 2023 for the "Stuck in a Loop" themed UNCC 2023 Spring 49hr Game Jam, OrbitaLoop is an arcade-like pixel art game where you must see how long you can last in a ship stuck in orbit during an endless barrage of alien attacks.

April 2023

New Group Member: Kylie

Sweet Frontier accepts a new member in the form of the artist Kylie for aid in development for the UNCC 2023 Spring 49hr Game Jam.

  
 

February 2023

(NEW GAME) NBS-LRR releases.

Releasing on February 5, 2023 for the "Roots" themed 2023 Global Game Jam, NBS-LRR is an arcade-like pixel art game where you must send out tree spirits to defend against the evil termites.
   
2022

November 2022

(NEW GAME) Drift No More releases.

Releasing on November 6, 2022 for the "Drifting" themed UNCC 2022 Fall 49hr Game Jam, DO NOT TOUCH THIS LITERAL BIOHAZARD UNDER ANY CONDITIONS.

  
 

September 2022

NAME CHANGE: group Project Prince is now Sweet Frontier

On September 30, 2022, the group formed for the production of Project Prince names itself Sweet Frontier.

September 2022

New Group Members: Jus Liu and Smit Sakariya
Departure of Group Members: Robert Battle and Mohammad Nagda

Sweet Frontier accepts new members in the form of the programmer Smit Sakariya and artist (later programmer) Jus Liu for aid in development following the UNCC 2022 49er Kickoff Game Jam.
After the aforementioned jam, Robert Battle and Mohammad Nagda leave.

  
 

September 2022

(NEW GAME) Project Prince releases.

Releasing on September 23, 2022 for the "Platformer" themed UNCC 2022 49er Kickoff Game Jam, Project Prince follows the humble servant Silas on a pixel art filled adventure to save Prince Daniel, who was kidnapped by the evil Prince of Gloomwood.
   

August 2022

group Project Prince initial formation with members:
Joey Parker, Leigh Dubiel, De'Shon McNeil, Mohammad Nagda, Robert Battle

August 26, 2022. The "Platformer" themed UNCC 2022 49er Kickoff Game Jam, has just begun, and a new team has begun to form for the purpose of developing what would later be known as Project Prince
