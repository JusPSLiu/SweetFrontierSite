OrbitaLoop

 You're the last ship in the fleet, trying to survive as the evil aliens from the HD 219134 star system come after you, sending barrages of asteroids and plasma bullets. You're the last human ship left, and all seems hopeless.


 They've taken out your impulse engines, leaving you adrift in orbit around Kepler 22b, above the remains of the former colony and military base. The aliens only have to go a mere 640 light years further to reach Earth, and wipe out the rest of the human population.


 Death is an inevitability, but the longer you stall it, the longer the rest of humanity will be able to live.


 How long can you hold out before then?
